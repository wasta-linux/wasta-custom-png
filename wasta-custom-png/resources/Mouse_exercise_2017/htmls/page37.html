Words are normally black on white background.

Highlighting turns backgrounds into a different color. Like this white sentence with a dark background.


Here is how to highlight:

  1. Place the tip of the arrow in front of the words to highlight. When you see the arrow change to an I-bar,
  2. Hold the left mouse button down.
  3. Drag the I-bar across the words.
  4. When background behind words are a different color, they're highlighted.
